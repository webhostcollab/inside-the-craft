Inside the Craft

The moments behind how it's made

What It Is

Not behind-the-scenes.
Not film school.

Each episode is led by the people behind the work,
breaking down how they pulled off one standout moment.

How the most memorable moments in film are actually made.

Filmmaking, without the filter.

Why It
Works
  • Extends engagement beyond launch
  • Positions Netflix as a champion of filmmaking
  • Reveals the craft behind the work
  • Hooks instantly and holds attention
Format
One moment. One team. One breakdown.
Each episode (5 - 8 minutes)
Introduced by David Fincher.
Led by the people behind the work.

01. Opening

The finished result — big, immediate, undeniable. A man is launched through a windshield. Glass explodes. Impact feels real.

02. The Setup

Fincher frames the moment and introduces the team

03. The Walkthrough

The department head walks us through how their team pulled it off

04. The Reveal

The moment, now understood

What Makes It
Different

These are the moments people rewind. Now they understand how they were pulled off.

We're not explaining filmmaking. We're showing how it actually gets done.

Audiences are already leaning in. Sports documentaries have proven the appetite. Filmmaking is just as layered, but no one has approached it like this.

It puts the spotlight on the people behind the work — the ones who make these moments possible.

A format others will follow. Netflix defines it first.
